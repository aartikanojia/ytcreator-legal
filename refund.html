Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 12 April 2026

1. Overview

This Refund & Cancellation Policy explains how YTCreator handles subscription cancellations and refund requests for subscriptions purchased through the Android app. Android subscriptions are billed and managed through Google Play.

2. Subscription Plans

YTCreator currently offers the following plans:

  • Free Plan – No payment required. Access to basic features.
  • Pro Monthly – Billed monthly. Access to Pro features.

3. Cancellation Policy

  • If you subscribed through Google Play, cancel your subscription from the Google Play Store subscription settings.
  • Cancellations take effect at the end of the current billing cycle.
  • After cancellation, you will continue to have access to Pro features until the end of the period you have already paid for.
  • No further renewal charges will be made after a Google Play cancellation takes effect.

Deleting your YTCreator account does not automatically cancel your Google Play subscription. For billing cancellations, use Google Play. For account deletion or support, email girlsmilewith.kanojia@gmail.com.

4. Refund Policy

  • Refund requests must be submitted within 7 days of the original payment date.
  • Approved refunds will be processed within 5–7 business days.
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method used at the time of purchase.
  • Partial refunds are not provided for unused portions of a subscription period.

5. Eligible Refund Cases

Refunds may be considered in the following situations:

  • Accidental or duplicate payment for the same subscription.
  • You were charged after your Google Play cancellation should have taken effect.
  • The App was completely non-functional due to a technical issue on our end for a sustained period.
  • An unauthorised transaction was reported within 7 days of the charge.

6. Non-Refundable Cases

Refunds will not be provided in the following cases:

  • You changed your mind after purchasing.
  • You did not use the Pro features during the subscription period.
  • The refund request is submitted more than 7 days after the payment date.
  • Your account was terminated due to a violation of our Terms of Service.

8. How to Request a Refund

Send an email to girlsmilewith.kanojia@gmail.com with:

• Subject: Refund Request – [Your Registered Email]
• Your registered email address
• Date of payment
• Payment amount
• Reason for refund request

We will respond within 2 business days and process approved refunds within 5–7 business days.

9. Payment Processor

For the Android app, subscription payments are processed by Google Play. We are not responsible for delays in refund processing caused by banks, card issuers, Google Play, or payment networks after a refund has been initiated through the applicable billing provider.
