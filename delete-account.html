Request Account Deletion

Last updated: 9 April 2026

1. How to request deletion

If you want to delete your YTCreator account and associated data, please send an email request to girlsmilewith.kanojia@gmail.com.

Suggested subject line:
Account Deletion Request - [Your Registered Email]

2. What to include

To help us locate your YTCreator account quickly, please include:

  • Your registered email address
  • Your channel name
  • Any other identifying information that helps us locate your account

3. What happens next

Once we receive your request, we will review it and process deletion of your YTCreator account and associated data within a reasonable timeframe.

Some information may still be retained where required for legal, fraud-prevention, security, dispute-resolution, or payment compliance purposes.

4. YouTube and Google access

You can also revoke YTCreator's access to your Google or YouTube account at any time from your Google Account permissions page.
