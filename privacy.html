Privacy Policy

Last updated: 4 April 2026

1. Overview

YTCreator ("we", "our", or "us") operates the YTCreator mobile application available on iOS and Android. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Name and email address (via Google Sign-In)
  • YouTube channel data (via YouTube Data API and YouTube Analytics API)
  • Device information (device type, OS version)
  • App usage data (features used, session duration)
  • Payment information (processed by Razorpay; we do not store card details)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide video analytics and performance insights
  • To personalise recommendations based on your channel data
  • To process subscription payments securely
  • To send important app updates and push notifications
  • To improve user experience and fix issues

4. YouTube API Services

YTCreator uses YouTube API Services provided by Google. By using YTCreator, you also agree to the YouTube Terms of Service and Google Privacy Policy.

We access YouTube data exclusively to provide insights, analytics, and creator-focused features inside the app. We do not share, sell, or transfer your YouTube data to any third party for advertising or any unrelated purpose.

5. Google OAuth

We use Google OAuth 2.0 to authenticate users securely. We do not store your Google password. Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device.

6. Payments

Payments for Pro subscriptions are processed by Razorpay. We do not store your credit/debit card details or banking information. All payment data is handled by Razorpay in accordance with PCI-DSS standards. For Razorpay's privacy practices, refer to Razorpay's Privacy Policy.

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google – OAuth authentication and YouTube API access
  • Firebase – Push notifications and analytics
  • Razorpay – Payment processing

Each of these services may collect and process data according to their own privacy policies.

8. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encrypted transmission (HTTPS/TLS), secure token storage, and access controls. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Some information may be retained where required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, or payment compliance reasons.
